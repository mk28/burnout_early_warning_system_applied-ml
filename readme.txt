In today’s highly competitive academic landscape, college students often face mounting
pressures from deadlines, assignments, exams, and personal obligations. This constant pressure
can lead to burnout — a state of emotional, physical, and mental exhaustion caused by prolonged
stress.
Unfortunately, burnout among students is often underdiagnosed and untreated until it leads to
severe consequences like academic failure, anxiety, or depression.
Recognizing the early signs of burnout can help students take preventive action before reaching
critical stages.
This project proposes a Burnout Early Warning System (BEWS) that leverages Machine
Learning (ML) to predict students' burnout risk based on behavioral, physiological, and
academic parameters, offering timely interventions and personalized recommendations.
